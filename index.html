ABOUT OUR CAMPAIGN

Every year hundreds of

thousands of dogs are

abandoned to shelters

Many of them wont be lucky enough to be adopted and will face the risk of being put down.

It does not have to be this way. The solution is pet adoption from shelters like the ones you'll be supporting during the Good Dog Walk to Freedom campaign.

Join thousands of other like-minded dog lovers this July as you directly impact the lives and well-being of mans best friend one step at a time.
